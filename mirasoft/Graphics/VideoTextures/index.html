Video Textures

Concept:
Video textures are arbitrarily long videos constructed by transitioning between the similar frames. Several transition points can exist within a single collection of frames and the decision whether to transition or not adds an obfuscation layer to the transitions. This artifact works best with continuous phenomena as exhibited here with a few firey samples.
