Life

Controls:
Left Click: Toggle cell on/off
G: Toggle step/continuous simulation
R: Randomly populate grid
C: Clear grid
Space: Step/Simulate

Concept:
One of the most famous cellular automata: the Game of Life. Though the ruleset is fairly simplistic, many patterns emerge that exhibit stable or otherwise interesting behavior ranging from gliders that glide across the grid to large patterns that slowly slide along while also spawning gliders themselves.
