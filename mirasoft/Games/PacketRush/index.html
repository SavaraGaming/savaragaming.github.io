Packet Rush

Controls:
Click (Tap): Navigate Menus
Click (Tap) and Drag Up or Down: Change Lanes
Objective: Avoid the viruses!

Concept:
This is a quick, single-action mobile game about traveling the internet. Some functionality may be limited or missing in this particular build. For example, the ad screen gets skipped on the WebGL build and the DLC screen is just for show. This was a team project for my undergraduate Senior Design class. I programmed the main interaction of swiping to change lanes.
